[h1][previewimg=9122091;sizeOriginal,floatLeft;note_en.png][/previewimg][b]Steam Groups[/b] are made to connect users who share the same interests. Each group has its own set of rules, violating them will result in a ban. A user who is breaking the rules may be subject to being banned from the group [b]discussions[/b] or the group's own [b]chatroom[/b]. They can also be [b]kicked[/b] from the group.[/h1]
