The [b]Steam Community Ban[/b] is a ban which restricts access to the Steam Community features for breaking the [url=https://support.steampowered.com/kb_article.php?ref=4045-USHJ-3810]Rules and Guidelines[/url] or as a result of a lock placed on your account.
[previewicon=10519342;sizeOriginal,inline;communityban_profile_rednotification.png][/previewicon]
A Steam Community Ban is generally issued by a Community Moderator or a Steam Support Staff Member. [previewimg=9122108;sizeOriginal,floatRight;m2.png][/previewimg][b]This ban may be associated with, but not limited to the following actions:[/b]
[list]
[*] The violation of any paragraph of the [url=https://store.steampowered.com/subscriber_agreement/]Steam Subscriber Agreement[/url] and [url=https://store.steampowered.com/online_conduct/]Steam Online Conduct[/url];
[*] The use of graphic or otherwise inappropriate avatars, screenshots, artwork etc.;
[*] Spamming the community;
[*] Inappropriate language;
[*] Harassing other users;
[*] Continuing to post moderated content;
[*] Posting content related to racism or other forms of bigotry.
[/list]

[h1]Here is an example of a ban issued by Steam Support for spamming in the community.[/h1]

Steam Support sends an [previewicon=10394724;sizeOriginal,inline;communityban_accountalert.png][/previewicon] to the offending user:
[previewicon=10405733;sizeOriginal,inline;communityban_supportmessage.png][/previewicon]

[previewimg=10519270;sizeOriginal,inline;communityban_moderatormessage_message.png][/previewimg] As well as the account alert, the user will also receive a notification in their [url=https://steamcommunity.com/my/moderatormessages/]“Community Moderation Messages”[/url] that details what content was against the rules and what was removed.


[h1][b]A standard community ban usually includes the following restrictions:[/b][/h1]
[previewimg=9122089;sizeOriginal,floatRight;forbidden.png][/previewimg][quote]The user's profile is hidden without the ”This profile is private” label;[/quote]
[previewimg=9122089;sizeOriginal,floatRight;forbidden.png][/previewimg][quote]The user is unable to participate in the Steam Discussions;[/quote]
[previewimg=9122089;sizeOriginal,floatRight;forbidden.png][/previewimg][quote]The user cannot leave comments in the activity feed of their own profile or in that of other users' profiles:
[previewicon=10394886;sizeOriginal,inline;communityban_profilecomments1.png][/previewicon][/quote]

[previewimg=9122089;sizeOriginal,floatRight;forbidden.png][/previewimg][quote][previewimg=10394774;sizeOriginal,floatRight;communityban_friendrequestban.png][/previewimg] The user will not be able to send group and friend requests, nor will they be able to accept them from other users.[/quote]

[previewimg=9122089;sizeOriginal,floatRight;forbidden.png][/previewimg][quote][previewimg=10519405;sizeThumb,floatRight;communityban_editprofileban1.png][/previewimg] [previewimg=10519407;sizeOriginal,floatRight;communityban_editprofileban2.png][/previewimg]The user is unable to edit their profile, i.e cannot change profile name, country, custom URL, avatar, summary, profile background, showcases, etc.




[/quote]
[previewimg=9122089;sizeOriginal,floatRight;forbidden.png][/previewimg][quote]The user is unable to see the number of common groups and friends with another user.[/quote]
[previewimg=9122089;sizeOriginal,floatRight;forbidden.png][/previewimg][quote]The user is unable to add other users to the ignored users list.[/quote]
[previewimg=9122089;sizeOriginal,floatRight;forbidden.png][/previewimg][quote]The user is unable to trade or use the Community Market.[/quote]
